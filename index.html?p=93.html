We aim to make the Camp Stella Puella experience accessible to all.

Day Camp is located at 305 Helena St., Dunnville, ON. Campers will benefit from a field-trip to Shared Harvest Community Farm, recreational swimming, and a social action initiative within the local community.

Day campers are placed in groups for age-appropriate programming. The camper to staff ratio is 6:1. During the week, day campers bring their own packed lunches and are encouraged to bring reusable water-bottles and containers. Regular camp hours are 9am to 4pm, however, pre-camp and after-camp services are available for an additional charge.

Leaders In Training Program:

Our Leader in Training (LIT) program is for campers aged 13-14. Rates for campers in this age group do not differ from our regular session rates, but these campers will be given the opportunity to play a valuable leadership role in the camp atmosphere. LITs will bond as a group and work as a team to contribute to workshops and activities run by the counselors of Camp Stella Puella. In addition, LITs will be offered intimate self-esteem and body image workshops designed for their age group. LITs also have the opportunity to participate in a one night overnight experience which includes canoeing, a campfire and reflection activities. Campers aged 13-14 years will automatically be enrolled in the LIT program. LITs receive community hours that can go towards their high school diploma.
