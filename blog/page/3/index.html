Let’s get down to business

Posted on February 18, 2012 by julia

Considering this summer will be Camp Stella Puella’s FIFTH year anniversary, we figured it was about time to have our First Annual CSP Retreat. And what better place to do it than where it all started, in Montreal with our dear mentor, Aunt Zard!

So here we have it, our team for the weekend in addition to a few special guests and advisors that we can’t wait to meet with!

Yasmin: Otherwise known as DJ Yaz Jazz, she is the logistical brain and executional eyes for efficiency
Mina: Passionate and detail-oriented, creative is her middle name and Marcel the Shell with Shoes her inspiration
Julia: Communications and media specialist, “J-Fal” keeps us awake, keeps it fresh
Aunt Zard: Chair of Board of Advisors, “AZ Yo” brings her wealth of experience in education, summer camps, and smoothies to the table
