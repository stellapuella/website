We aim to make the Camp Stella Puella experience accessible to all.

At Overnight Camp, we offer an extended Camp Stella Puella experience. With a unique balance between fun and learning, campers will treasure the enthusiasm and intimacy of the camp environment. Campers will be given the opportunity to develop their teamwork and leadership skills, while getting an in-depth look at social issues both locally and globally. Additionally, they will be given the opportunity to participate in confidence building workshops to foster personal awareness, growth, and expression.

At Overnight Camp, campers will get the ultimate camp experience by participating in special evening programs, ranging from campfires to talent shows. As well as gaining from special guest instructors, performers, and speakers; campers will discover their passion and ways to implement action in their own lives and communities.

Camp Stella Puella’s Overnight Camp is located on the shores of Lake Erie near Dunnville, Ontario. Using the traditional facilities at Camp Kienuka, we are able to create an intimate atmosphere with a maximum of 25 campers who will enjoy comfortable indoor facilities and plenty of outdoor space for activity. Campers will be provided with three wholesome, nutritious meals a day and snacks courtesy of their resident, professional chefs. The majority of the food served will be from local, organic and fairly traded sources.
